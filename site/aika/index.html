Hello! Our project is called 21 days. We give you change your life for the better within 21 days. The goal of our project is to help you acquire positive habits. But why 21 days?


Each of the participants will have a mentor. A mentor will help you with advice. A meeting with the mentor should occur every 3 days and you will be required to give an account of what has been done to each participant. For example, how many books have you read? Or how many miles did you run?

Theory of 21 days


As British scientists tell us, if you repeat a certain action systematically within twenty one days, it will become a habit and vice versa, you need 21 days to get rid of the habit. You want to quit smoking, endure 21 days without cigarettes, you want to start smoking, so try for 21 days. Charging is an obvious blessing, but it's not easy for me. Frankly, hard given through force. Therefore, I chose a simple complex for ass ABL + a couple of exercises for the press + medical gymnastics for the back, which the doctor ordered. A total of an hour. And she did it for 21 days in any weather, in any mood and with any well-being. It should be noted that well-being has improved, yes. Visually there are no special changes. To the touch, they say, "oh, yes, cool, do not stop there are positive changes. Was it easy? No! Every day I forced myself, delayed the moment of beginning, suffered, but then I gathered my ass in a handful of fists and did it. in the morning, when I got up, then morning, I did it in the evening — I almost died, it was much harder. Has it become a habit? I can not say. But I don’t want to rush straight to the press bench. For myself, I decided that I can not do today. And most likely with joy I will not. Then I add approaches, loads and I will again, because after the workout I feel better. From the very positive: therapeutic exercises for the back have become painless. The first time I did them with tears, although nothing like that, all sorts of stretching, holding, lying on the rollers. During this 21 days I have never had a backache, although I don’t even remember when it was such a miracle. Bottom line: the habits did not appear, the desire to do exercises, too, no, but based on my well-being, I will continue.

First step

At first you should choose your plan. In this step your goal is ti listen your heart and choose three things in your life that you want to change.

Second step

In this step you should contact with your mentor. He will help you in building plan for 21 days. Plan should contain three main parts. Each part is for 1 week. In the end of each week you have to show what you did

Third step

Start improving your life!!! But do not forget to contact with your mentor every three days and get new tasks!
